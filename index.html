After starting out in the lower ranks, I worked my way up and held a number of mid-level positions spanning all departments which culminated in being promoted to Operations Manager which was in reality the Cinema & I.T Systems Manager. This title afforded me the relative freedom to implement company-wide changes which untimatly transformed Zeffiilies into the hugely successfull digitially driven business it is today.
